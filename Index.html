About

The research project that produced this website aimed at determining how intangible assets affected the capital structure of information technology companies that were publicly traded on the Philippine Stock Exchange between 2019 and 2023.



Purpose of the study

The objective of this research is to determine how effectively, in terms of debt ratio the capital structures of publicly traded information technology businesses on the Philippine Stock Exchange performed between 2019 and 2023.

It also aims to evaluate the intangible assets of publicly traded IT businesses in terms of the intangible asset sample, the intangible asset rate, and the book value of intangible assets. In keeping with the focus of the investigation,

Usually, the goal is to determine whether intangible assets significantly affect the capital structure.
